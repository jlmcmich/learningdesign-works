With graduate assistant **Liz McGlynn Bellamy**, I created a lesson planning template merging the **ACRL Framework for Information Literacy** with **Wiggins and McTighe's Understanding by Design** methodology. This approach enhanced the quality and consistency of information literacy instruction at UNC Libraries.

## What the Template Does

The template empowers instructors to:

- Deconstruct research assignments to identify required skills and knowledge
- Align learning objectives with information literacy threshold concepts
- Emphasize the "Big Idea" in lesson focus
- Develop authentic activities demonstrating student learning
- Create detailed learning plans addressing assignment-specific challenges

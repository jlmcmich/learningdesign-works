I led the development of online instructional videos for UNC Libraries focused on Information Literacy. The project drew inspiration from **narrative-based explanations for the core concepts of Information Literacy** and was designed to be flexible and easily adaptable for use in various learning environments.

## Production

I collaborated with graduate assistants to establish a streamlined workflow encompassing:

- Script writing
- Animation
- Audio recording
- Editing

The team employed software including Prezi, iMovie, Audacity, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop. Videos are hosted on UNC Libraries' YouTube channel.

## Impact

The video series **improved consistency** across library instruction sessions and motivated librarians to incorporate **increased active learning strategies**.

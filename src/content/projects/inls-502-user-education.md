I redesigned and taught a graduate course at UNC's School of Information & Library Science for librarians pursuing educational roles. The course provided **a comprehensive overview of instructional and educational design theory** while enabling students to design and implement evidence-based learning experiences.

## Key Course Components

### Learning Reflections

Students posted daily reflections on class content to the LMS — reinforcing learning, facilitating discussion, and developing educator capabilities.

### Learning Facilitator Days

Beginning in week three, students rotated leading class sessions on approved topics, developing lesson plans with instructor guidance and receiving feedback for improvement.

### Final Project

Students wrote personal **teaching philosophies** and created annotated lesson plans applying those philosophies to real educational scenarios.

I created a lesson planning template to address the increasing demand for technical workshops at SMU Libraries' **Scholarship & Research Services**.

The template is designed to be used by instructors with varying levels of instructional design experience, accommodating content experts from multiple disciplines — including faculty and IT professionals.

## Framework

The framework draws on two key instructional approaches:

- **Essential Questioning**
- **Backwards Design** principles

## Three-Stage Learning Sequence

1. **Pre-Workshop**
2. **Synchronous Learning Session**
3. **Post-Workshop Support Page**

This flexible approach provides a structured and efficient way for instructors to create impactful workshops.

SMU's Quality Enhancement Plan represents the university's comprehensive approach to improving retention and four-year graduation rates. The initiative focuses on advancing student academic success through progress toward degree completion.

## Role & Pillar

I served on the **First Year and Gateway Courses Pillar**, which aimed to enhance course design and instruction while reducing equity gaps among students.

## Key Strategies Implemented

The pillar developed three main approaches:

1. A nine-month **Course Redesign Initiative** targeting high-enrollment and gateway courses
2. A **Student Engagement Institute** providing faculty pedagogical development opportunities
3. Strategic placement of **experienced instructors** in foundational courses

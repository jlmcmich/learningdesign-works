Working with the [Scholarship & Research team](https://www.smu.edu/libraries/scholarship) at SMU Libraries and others from around campus, we developed early training, best practices, and policy recommendations for accommodating ChatGPT and other generative AI technologies in the college classroom.

## Areas of Work

- Resources for **ChatGPT in the classroom and instructional design**
- Writing for the [SMU Libraries blog](https://blog.smu.edu/smulibraries/2023/01/20/artificial-intelligence-and-the-research-paper-a-librarians-perspective/) on the teaching/learning affordances and challenges of AI
- Introductory workshops on Artificial Intelligence as part of SMU Libraries' Scholarship & Research workshop series
- An introduction to text mining series covering relevant Machine Learning topics, including Natural Language Processing
- Additional workshop series with other campus units, including small-group faculty meetings and round-table discussions

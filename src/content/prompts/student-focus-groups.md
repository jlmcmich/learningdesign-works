Simulates focus-group feedback evaluating how diverse learners approach course assignments.

## Group Composition

Three diverse college students with different academic motivations and learning patterns.

## Process

1. Request documents/description and context (learning objectives, assignment stakes).
2. Suggest four distinct approaches (including **Undirected Learning**).
3. For each approach: student-perspective explanation, learning-pattern alignment, framework-based rationale.
4. Provide two experiences per approach (positive and less positive) with student quotes.
5. Generate recommendations based on student experiences.

Creates effective discussion board prompts to foster higher-order thinking in online environments.

## Six Evidence-Based Prompting Strategies

1. **Brainstorm** — Divergent generation; analysis to synthesis to creation.
2. **Focal Question** — Students defend positions; evaluation and argumentation.
3. **Playground** — Interpretation and analysis of specific materials.
4. **Role-Playing Scenario** — Perspective-taking; application and decision-making.
5. **Structured Debate** — Assigned argumentation; evaluation and critique.
6. **Research & Integration** — Source evaluation and synthesis.

## Process

- **Phase 1** — Initiate and gather learning objectives and course content.
- **Phase 2** — Draft three distinct strategy options with rationale.
- **Phase 3** — Closed-loop revision and finalization.

## Final Output

- Student-Facing Language (Overview, Prompt, Expectations & Guidelines)
- Instructor Facilitation Guide
- Optional Canvas-Friendly HTML Version

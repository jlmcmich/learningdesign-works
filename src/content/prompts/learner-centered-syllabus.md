Drafts agency-oriented, student-centered syllabus language aligned with accessibility and equity best practices.

## Approach

Based on **Cruelty-Free Pedagogy** principles (Cheney & Stommel). Prioritizes positive tone, views students as trustworthy, and focuses on **support over punishment**.

## Policy Structure

- Practical explanation
- Learning benefit statement
- Encouragement for conversation
- Necessary guidelines explanation
- Emphasis on faculty partnership and student success

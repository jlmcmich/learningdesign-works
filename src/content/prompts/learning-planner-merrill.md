Develops lesson plans based on **Merrill's Principles of Instruction**, aligned with learning objectives.

## Merrill's Five Principles

1. **Task-centered** approach
2. **Activation** (engage prior knowledge)
3. **Demonstration** (provide models / examples)
4. **Application** (practice in authentic scenarios)
5. **Integration** (reflect and transfer learning)

## Process

1. Gather course details (topic, objectives, modality, time allocation).
2. Design a module using the five principles with specific recommendations.
3. Summarize the learning plan from the learner's perspective ("Students will…").
4. Explain the assessment plan aligned to MLOs.
5. Request feedback and adjust.

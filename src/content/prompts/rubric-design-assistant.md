Creates clear, detailed rubrics with specific criteria and performance-level descriptors.

## Six-Step Process

1. **Understand** the assignment and learning objectives.
2. **Determine** rubric type — analytic vs. holistic.
3. **Define** rubric components: task description, characteristics (rows), mastery levels (columns), performance descriptions.
4. **Consider** student success — transparency, actionability, alignment.
5. **Calibrate** and suggest uses (norming sessions, formative assessment).
6. **Solicit** feedback and revise.

## Design Constraints

- Clear and concise descriptions
- Mutually exclusive criteria
- Easy interpretation

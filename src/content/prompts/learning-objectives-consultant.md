Assists in crafting clear, measurable learning objectives using **Bloom's Taxonomy**. Works collaboratively through three phases.

## Workflow

### Phase 1: Initiation
Gathers essential context (course title, level, description) and additional contextual information.

### Phase 2: Draft & Propose
Creates 2–5 course learning objectives ordered from lower- to higher-order cognitive skills, with explanatory rationale.

### Phase 3: Refinement
Solicits specific feedback and revises objectives through closed-loop iteration.

## Output Format

- Final Course Learning Objectives
- Instructional Design Notes & Alignment Rationale
- Suggested Assessment Strategies
- Next Steps & Additional Tools

## Guiding Principles

1. **Hierarchy of Alignment** (Understanding by Design)
2. **Measurable vs. Conceptual Objectives**
3. **Prioritize Learning Goals** (Acquisition → Meaning-making → Transfer)
4. **CLOs as Future Design Inputs**
